GNOME Board of Directors Elections 2011

Date Wed 11 May 2011 Tags english

[caption id=”” align=”alignnone” width=”356” caption=”By Gwen’s River City Images”][/caption]

I can’t believe it’s been this long already, but it is time for yet another Board of Directors Election! Having had the opportunity to serve on the Board for these last 12 months, I want to encourage anyone who have the time and interest to improve the GNOME project to run for one of the seven spots on the Board of Directors! For more information on this, please read the official announcement here! A few important dates to keep in mind:

  • 2011-05-22: List of candidates closed
  • 2011-05-29: Voting Begins
  • 2011-06-12: Voting Ends
  • 2011-06-21: Challenges End

UPDATE: The picture displayed here is not in any shape or form a hint, indication, or suggestion of who one should vote for in any election, period! If you, however, feel strongly encouraged/compelled to do what pictures may suggest, please let me know as I will put a picture of what kind of gifts you can then be compelled to buy me for my birthday! :)
